Our organization specializes in working with Poodles, known for their hypoallergenic qualities, making them ideal companions for individuals who suffer from allergies. We have carefully chosen Poodles for their gentle nature, intelligence, and suitability as emotional support animals, particularly for people who benefit from the calming and therapeutic presence of dogs. Over the past 10 years, we have witnessed firsthand the profound impact that these wonderful animals can have on individuals struggling with various mental health challenges.

Our Poodles are part of a mobile team that we bring to different locations, such as care facilities, hospitals, community centers, and even private homes, to provide support and companionship to those in need. These visits have proven to be incredibly uplifting, offering comfort and joy to people who may be feeling isolated, anxious, or overwhelmed. Whether it’s a one-on-one session or a group visit, our Poodles have an exceptional ability to connect with people, bringing smiles and a sense of calm that is invaluable to mental well-being.

In addition to these visits, we also provide a specialized walking service. This unique experience involves a small, guided group walk led by one of our team members, accompanied by our Poodles. These walks offer participants a chance to reconnect with nature, enjoy the beauty of the English countryside, and engage in gentle physical activity—all of which have been shown to have positive effects on mental health. By encouraging people to step outside and explore scenic routes, we aim to create a nurturing environment that fosters mental rejuvenation, social connection, and overall well-being.

Through both our therapeutic visits and walking services, we strive to make a meaningful difference in people's lives by leveraging the therapeutic power of animals and the natural world. Our Poodles are more than just pets; they are companions, caregivers, and friends who bring comfort, positivity, and healing to those who need it most.
